Project Overview & Vision

PhysicianConnect is a simple tool built just for doctors. It helps them manage their time, prepare for appointments, and follow up with patients more easily. Instead of juggling paper notes, phone calls, or multiple systems, everything lives in one place—saving time and improving care quality.

Doctors set working hours, block off breaks, and see patient‑booked visits appear instantly. Pre‑visit notes give them context, while post‑visit tools capture notes, prescriptions, referrals, and reminders. Built to be fast, focused, and secure, PhysicianConnect strips away the cruft of heavyweight EMRs so small‑clinic physicians can spend more face‑time with patients.

Intended Users

Primary users are general practitioners and specialists in small‑ to medium‑sized clinics who need a lightweight alternative to bulky hospital EMR systems.

Major Functionality

  • Calendar-based schedule management with availability blocks
  • Instant syncing of patient-booked appointments
  • Pre-visit notes & history at a glance
  • Medication prescription with dosage instructions
  • Notification & alert system for doctors and patients
  • Post-visit documentation: notes, prescriptions, and referrals
  • Live messaging/chat between doctors and patients
  • Billing & invoicing with patient-level detail
  • Profile management for staff and users
  • Secure, role-based data separation for doctors & patients
  • Receptionist view with limited admin access
  • Patient feedback & ratings system
